Split field

Optionally spit into multiple visualisations based on the value of this key.

Example

In this example dataset:

Size Year Green Blue
Small 2010 10 15
Small 2015 12 17
Medium 2010 30 35
Medium 2015 32 37
Large 2010 60 70
Large 2015 62 72

If you split on the Year field, you would get 2 graphs/tables that look like this:

2010

Size Year Green Blue
Small 2010 10 15
Medium 2010 30 35
Large 2010 60 70

2015

Size Year Green Blue
Small 2015 12 17
Medium 2015 32 37
Large 2015 62 72
